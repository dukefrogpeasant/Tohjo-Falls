Pokémon Box Ruby and Sapphire is a storage game for the Gamecube. Unlike most games, it was exclusive to the NYC Pokémon Center and pokemoncenter's site in the USA, and in Europe was only sold together with Pokémon Colosseum. This also sadly means that it was really rare in english-speaking regions...
Special Pokémon can be obtained by depositing select amounts of pokemon into the program. You can find them on this page!>
Players must have 100 Pokémon caught in the Pokédex before they can withdraw pokemon that arent deposited by them.
