Pokémon Box: Ruby and Sapphire has several exclusive Pokémon to it. These are Pokémon you can obtain in Hoenn, but they have special moves they otherwise would NEVER learn.
They are:

Swablue with False Swipe (Obtained when depositing: None(start of game))
Zigzagoon with ExtremeSpeed (Obtained when depositing: 100 Pokémon)
Skitty with Pay Day (Obtained when depositing: 500 Pokémon)
Pichu with Surf (Obtained when depositing: 1499 Pokémon, which is 1 away from 1500)
