Pokemon Channel has a good handful of minigames taken over from the Pokemon Mini systems. Japan has more than the english/international versions, but i will only name the international ones here.
They are:

Snorlax's Lunch Time: Snorlax's Lunch Time is a game where you control Snorlax and tell him when to eat. If you eat a Pichu, then it's game over for you! Just eat at a certain beat (like a beat of a second). When Pichu appears on the screen, just wait until he's gone and continue the beat. This one's actually EXCLUSIVE to Channel, because it is a CANCELED Mini game!
Slowking's Judge: Judge whether the ball landed IN or OUT of the line. The more points you get right, the tougher it is. Wait until the ball hits the ground.
Chansey's Dribble: Kick the soccerball all the way to the other side. Just move Chansey in front of the ball to kick.
Pokemon Pinball Petit: Play different kinds of Pokemon Pinball. This game allows you to play as Diglett (or Pikachu if you completed the Quest mode). As a tip, hold diglett down while the ball is falling into place!
Puzzle Collection Petit: Play different kinds of puzzle games. There are 3 kinds of puzzles. Each puzzle has something unique about it. Think before you act, because you can mess up really easily.
Pikachu's Rocket Start: Press "A" as fast as you can when you feel your controller rumble.
