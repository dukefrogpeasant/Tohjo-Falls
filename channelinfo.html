Pokemon Channel

Pokemon Channel is a Pokemon television channel that has lost some parts to it's popular show, Pichu Bros. Now it's up to you and Pikachu to help find the missing parts. In this game, you can communicate with Pikachu, and take Pikachu out to see other Pokemon. As you find the clips, you will be able to view them. You will also be able to take scenes and color them the way you want. You could pretty much see it as a sequel to Hey You, Pikachu.
