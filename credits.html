Credits and disclaimer


Tohjo Falls was a Pokémon fansite that ran from ~2000 to around 2005. It was ran by Matt and co-authored by Raichu, as far as I know. It ran on Brinkster hosting, then got hosted for a bit by Pokedavid. Afterwards, it was hosted by Sakurablossoms for a little while in 2002, before getting hosted by Pokémon Sky. and then on .cjb, however CJB seems to be a link-through site and not a proper host.

This serves as a reconstruction and eventual continuation of the site. Data is pulled from the Wayback Machine for the 2005 layout from the .cjb. directory, and site content is pulled from the .pokemonsky. site and brinkster site.

This site was relaunched with the permission of Raichu, one of the original people who wrote for Tohjo Falls.
