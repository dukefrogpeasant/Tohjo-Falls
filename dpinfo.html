Diamond/Pearl Info

Release Dates

Japan: September 28, 2006
North America: April 22, 2007
Australia: April 26, 2007
Europe: July 27, 2007

Pokemon Diamond and Pearl are the fourth generation of Pokemon games, and the first main-line games for the Nintendo DS.

The game makes use of the DS its touchscreen, time and internet connection. The overworld also is now in 3D!

New Stuff

  • Your starters are Chimchar, a fire type; Piplup, a water type; and Turtwig, a grass type.
  • 104 new Pokemon introduced
  • The game uses day/night again like in Gold/Silver
  • Types are no longer the deciding factor on if a move uses Attack or Special Attack.
  • There's only two fire types before you beat the elite 4, so if you want a strong fire type pick Chimchar, otherwise you can only get Ponyta.
  • You live in Twinleaf Town which is near a lake where a legendary pokemon resides.
  • Eight new gym leaders with different types of Pokemon
  • New evolutions for older Pokemon such as Roselia(gets a baby and evo), Lickitung and Yanma
