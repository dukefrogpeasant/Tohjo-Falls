Okay so, to get a Manaphy Egg, you need to have a copy of Pokémon Ranger and Diamond and Pearl. You first need to beat Pokémon Ranger to get access to the Ranger Net. Once you have access to the Ranger Net, you need to enter a password(the password screen can be unlocked by going to Ranger Net and holding R, X, and Left at the same time).

enter the password "P8M2-9D6F-43H7" for the US version or "Mg35-Cpb8-4FW8" for the UK version.

After entering the password, you get a mission. finish the mission to get the egg.

So now after you're done the mission, you can recheck Ranger Net and find a new option called "Check the Egg." This will also let you send it to Diamond or Pearl version. It also gives instructions on how to do the transfer, so just follow those.

After you transfer the egg to Diamond or Pearl, you need to walk around with it in your party to hatch it. Just like any other egg, it will take 5120 steps to hatch. Once it hatches, you will have your very own Manaphy! Also, if you have a Manaphy, you can breed it with a Ditto to get a Phione in Diamond/Pearl! It has the same stats as Glalie but it's a water type.
