Emerald Info

Release Dates
JP: Sept 16, 2004 NA: May 1, 2005 AU: Jun 9, 2005 EU: Oct 21, 2005

Pokémon Emerald is the upper version of Pokémon Ruby and Sapphire. It has various differences from its predecessors. It also alters the story of Ruby and Sapphire a bit and introduces various new things, fixes some things and solves other problems with Ruby and Sapphire. Also, MOVING SPRITES ARE BACK!

New Stuff

  • You can rematch gym leaders
  • Deoxys gets a Speed form
  • Wallace becomes Champion
  • Trainers can be rematched with Match call
  • Animated Pokémon sprites
  • Safari Zone got updated
  • Battle Frontier
  • New water type gym leader
