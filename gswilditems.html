Wild Pokémon: Items

  Rarely while training, you will find yourself catching a Pokémon with an item.  You can take these items without catching the Pokémon using an attack called Thief.  With Thief, assuming that the Pokémon using the attack is not carrying an item, the attacker will steal the Wild Pokémon's item, and equip it.  However, there's no way of knowing if the Pokémon has at item until you've used Thief or caught it.  Use the table below to aid you, though, and you'll at least know who has the items, and how often!
  Pokémon with a * by their name cannot actually be caught in the wild, thus cannot actually be found with the item.
