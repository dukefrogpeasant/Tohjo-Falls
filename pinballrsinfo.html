A sequel to Pinball for the Gameboy Color! This new version of the game features updated and modern graphics and better mechanics. However, this game does not feature a rumble pak unlike its predecessor. It DOES allow vibrations to go through a Gamecube controller if you are playing it on the Gamecube!

You have 2 boards to choose from. A Ruby Board and a Sapphire Board. These boards both have multiple Pokémon in them to help or thwart your attempts to capture every single Pokémon in the help or thwart your attempts to capture every single Pokémon in the area that you are in, so you will have to switch boards every so often. theres exclusives for both the ruby and the Sapphire boards! Their layout is also a little different so the gameplay doesnt become stale!

This game features a whole total of 205 Pokémon to capture, containing the entirity of the Hoenn Pokedex, the Johto starters and the Kanto Pokémon Aerodactyl. It even features various bonus rounds featuring these Pokémon and even includes a whole new mechanic to allow for the obtaining and hatching of Pokémon through eggs! Careful though because these move around and may flee from you.
