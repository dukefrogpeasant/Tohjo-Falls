Pokémon Platinum has a few differences from Pokémon Diamond and Pearl. It fixes a lot of issues DP had, thankfully.
  • You can now go up on the Pokégear instead of only down.
  • Giratina is the boxart now, and Dialga/Palkia can be caught in post game.
  • A lot of the Pokémon that gained evolutions appear in the dex now, but Misdreavus and Murkrow aren't obtainable. A few of these are in the Safari Zone such as Tangela and Yanma.
  • A lot of the overworld looks better now, like the resort area now having tiled instead of grass flooring outside, or the starting town looking far more snowy.
  • There are more fire types, such as Houndour, in the Pokédex now, making it much more easy to actually get a fire type instead of being just locked to Chimchar and Ponyta.
  • Some of the gyms were fixed up to make more sense. Fantina's gym went from a math puzzle(?!) to a place with flashlights, and is now the third gym.
  • Gardenias gym also isn't a forest maze anymore, but now a flower clock.
  • Maylenes gym also was fixed a little so it's actually punching bags instead of wood panels.
  • You can buy more than one single honey at once.
  • Hypnosis accuracy is back down to 60. If you're fighting against a DP player, it'll stay 60.
  • THE BATTLE FRONTIER IS BACK.
