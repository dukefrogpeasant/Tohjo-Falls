Pokémon Platinum introduces a few new forms to some gen 4 Pokémon.
These are:
Giratina Origin Form; ability becomes Levitate and it becomes more offensive bc the defenses and offenses swap places. Giratina needs to hold a Griseous Orb for this, a item you catch it with in the Distortion World
Sky Form Shaymin; Shaymin with the flying type. SUPER fast. It also has Serene grace so it's a very annoying pokemon to deal with. You need a Gracidea Flower for this.
Rotom Forms; Rotom has gotten multiple new forms. Each of these forms learns a exclusive move and they all have better stats than the normal Rotom you catch. You need a event key for this.
