CHEATS

Increase Difficulty:

  • At the difficulty selection screen, hold Z while pressing L, L, A, B.
  • OR Complete the Hard mode.

Highest Difficulty:

  • At the difficulty selection screen, hold Z while pressing R, L, A, B.
  • OR Complete the Increased Difficulty mode (above).

Increased Speed:

  • At the title screen, hold Z while pressing B, A, L, L.

Extra Trainers:

  • At the trainer selection screen, hold down Z, L & R on both controllers. This will reveal a trainer called "?".
  • OR Complete the Spa Service Game.

Unlock All Of The Puzzle University Games:

  • At the title screen, hold Z while pressing A, B, R, A, A, B, R, A.

Annoy Your Opponent:

  • Repeatedly press A during a 2 Player Match.

Mewtwo Stage:
Player 2 will be Mewtwo:

  • At the trainer selection screen, hold Z while pressing B, Up, L, B, A, START, A, UP, R.
  • OR Complete the Highest Difficulty mode.
