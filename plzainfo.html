Legends: ZA Info

Release Dates
Worldwide: Oct. 16, 2025

Pokémon Legends Z-A is the second game in the Pokémon Legends series. It follows Lumiose City, 5 years after the climax of the original games. Additionally, Mega Evolutions are back after their disappearance from the games starting in Gen 8.
You are a tourist visiting Lumiose when Taunie/Urbain wants you to film a promotion shoot for hotel Z, but you eventually get tangled into stuff when your bag gets stolen by a Pancham.

The tutorial takes a unfunnily long time, but after that, it's pretty much smooth sailing.
The game introduces 24 new Mega Evolutions.
