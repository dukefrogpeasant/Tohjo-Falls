Pokémon Legends: ZA regulations


There are competitive regulations/seasons for online battles, alongside with special rewards when you reach certain ranks (kalos starter mega stones). From the looks of it, each Season takes 20 days.

Season 1/Regulation A
Time period: October 16, 2025 to November 5, 2025
Special Rewards: Greninjite, after reaching Rank K
Pokémon Pool: All Pokémon except Legendary Pokémon.
Bans: Mewtwo, Xerneas, Mega Chesnaught, Mega Delphox, Yveltal, Zygarde, Diancie, Hoopa, Volcanion


Season 2/Regulation B
Time period: November 6, 2025 - November 26, 2025
Special Rewards: Greninjite, after reaching Rank Y, Delphoxite, after reaching rank S.
Pokémon Pool: All Pokémon except Mythical Pokémon and Mewtwo.
Restrictions: Mewtwo, Diancie, Hoopa, Volcanion, Mega Chesnaught.
Unbanned Pokémon: Xerneas, Yveltal, Mega Delphox
