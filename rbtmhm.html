TM/HM List

01 MEGA PUNCH-very strong fighting attack
02 RAZOR WIND-very strong flying attack that takes to turns to complete
03 SWORDS DANCE-increases attack strength
04 WHIRLWIND-in wild Pokemon battles, it will automatically end the battle
05 MEGA KICK-very strong fighting attack
06 TOXIC-poison attack that badly posions enemy,where on each turn the poison's damage increases
07 HORN DRILL-If successful, the enemy is automatically defeated (only 20% chance of it hitting though
08 BODY SLAM-strond attack with a chance of paralyzing the enemy
09 TAKE DOWN-strong attack that will do a lot of damage but you will recieve 1/4 the damage the enemy recieved
10 DOUBLE EDGE-strong attack in which the player takes damage equal to 1/4 what the enemy recieves.
11 BUBBLEBEAM-water attack with a chance of lowering the enemy's speed
12 WATER GUN-water attack
13 ICE BEAM-strong ice attack that has a chance of freezing the enemy
14 BLIZZARD-the best ice attack with a chance of freezing the enemy
15 HYPER BEAM-very strong attack but the player loses the next turn
16 PAY DAY-attack where after the battle ends the winner will recieve a small amount of money
17 SUBMISSION-strong attack but the player recieves 1/4 of damage inflicted
18 COUNTER-The player inflicts double the damage they recieved on their last turn, but it only works against physical attacks
19 SEISMIC TOSS-the amount of damage inflicted is equal to the level of the player's Pokemon
20 RAGE-attack where the player's Pokemon's attack points increase and keeps attacking until the battle ends
21 MEGA DRAIN-grass attack that absorbs health from the enemy equal to half of the attack damage
22 SOLARBEAM-strongest grass attack, takes two turns
23 DRAGON RAGE-strong dragon attack, takes 40 health from the enemy
24 THUNDERBOLT-electric attackthat paralyzes the enemy
25 THUNDER-strongest electric attack with a chance of paralyzing the enemy
26 EARTHQUAKE-very strong ground attack with no effect on flying Pokemon
27 FISSURE-strongest ground attack where is successful the enemy is automatically defeated. no effect on flying Pokemon
28 DIG-strong ground attack that takes two turns but while it is underground it is immune to attacks
29 PSYCHIC-strongest psychic attack with a chance of decreasing the enemy's special
30 TELEPORT-in battles with wild Pokemon, automatically ends battle
31 MIMIC-psychic attack where the Pokemon can use one of the enemy's attacks
32 DOUBLE TEAM-raises your Pokemon's evade
33 REFLECT-reduces damage recieved by half. works only on physical attacks (aka normal and fighting moves)
34 BIDE-Pokemon loses 2-3 turns, but then attacks with double the damage dealt
35 METRONOME-produces random attacks
36 SELF DESTRUCT-inflicts much damage but Pokemon faints
37 EGG BOMB-normal attack
38 FIRE BLAST-strongest fire attack with a good chance of burning the enemy
39 SWIFT-normal attack that never misses (except against ghosts)
40 SKULL BASH-strong normal attack that takes two turns
41 SOFTBOILED-Pokemon recoversone half of maximum hit points
42 DREAM EATER-strong psychic attack that can only work when the enemy is asleep
43 SKY ATTACK-strongest flying attack that takes two turns
44 REST- Pokemon sleeps for two turns and recovers all health
45 THUNDER WAVE-electric attack that paralyzes enemy
46 PSYWAVE-psychic attack where the damage dealt to enemy equals 1 and 1/2 times your Pokemon's experience level
47 EXPLOSION-very strong attack that destroys enemy usually in one hit but your Pokemon faints
48 ROCK SLIDE-strong rock attack
49 TRI-ATTACK-normal attack
50 SUBSTITUTE-Pokemon clones itself, each clone takes 1/4 the original's health and fight automatically

*HMs*
01 CUT-normal attack that hacks down bushes and is between scratch and slash in power
02 FLY-flying attack that takes two turns but the Pokemon is immune to attack while in the air and allows you to fly to places instead of walk
03 SURF-water attack that allows you to swim across water, is second-most-powerful water attack
04 STRENGTH-normal attack that can also move boulders
05 FLASH-electric attack that lowers enemy's accuracy and lights up dark area
