---White City---
***Stadium***
The Stadium is where you get to prove your Pokemon skills by winning the Pokemon Cups.
***GB Tower***
Use the Transfer Pak to play your Game Boy Pokemon games on the Nintendo 64.
***My Room***
You can see what the room of your Gold/Silver character looks like. You must have the Transfer Pak to use this option.
***Pokemon Lab***
This is Professor Oak's laboratory. He can help you check your Pokedex, organize your items, and trade Pokemon.
***Gym Leader Castle***
Battle your way through the castle defeating the Gym Leaders and their friends.
***Mini-Games***
Play mini-games involving some new Pokemon.
***Free Battle***
Battle with your friends. Up to four players can fight.
***Pokemon Academy***
Earl's Pokemon Academy is here. Earl can teach you different things about Pokemon.
---Battle Now!---
Select this mode to quickly go to a battle. You'll be randomly selected a group of Level 40 Pokemon. Select your difficulty and fight.
---Event Battle---
This is a two-player mode. Play by the rules of the regular cups or devise your own set of rules.
---Mystery Gift---
Visit the girl in Goldenrod City Department Store to open this mode. Now a girl will give you items, but you can only receive one a day.

===Mini-Games===
Select the Mini-Games selection on the map and you can choose from four options.Play A Mini-Game: Select a mini-game and play for fun. Can play with up to four players.Mini-Game Champion: Play against three other players for coins by playing the mini-games.1P Quiz: Answer as many questions as you can before time runs out. Quiz For All: Multi-player quiz. Get 10 right to win.
---Play A Mini-Game---
Begin by choosing how many players will play. The rest will be computer controlled. Next, select the difficulty of the computer intelligence. Now select your game.
***Gutsy Golbat***
Get as many hearts as you can before the end of the level to win. Ram the other Golbats to steal their hearts but watch out because they'll be after you too. Avoid the Magnetons because you'll lose hearts if they shock you. Controls:Control Stick: MoveA Button: Fly UpTips: Try to ram the other Golbats as often as possible into the Magnetons to steal their hearts. Fly far above or below the others so they can't get your hearts. When you see a Magneton, quickly get to the center of the screen. ***Topsy-Turvy***
You'll control Hitmontops here trying to knock each other out of the ring. Knock the others out five times to win. Controls: Control Stick: MoveA Button: Spin AttackTips: Use your Spin Attack to knock your opponents out. Wait until they are close to the edge of the arena and use it. ***Clear Cut Challenge***
Here you'll either take control of a Pinsir or a Scyther. Logs will drop in front of you with a white line on it. Cut it as close to the white line as you can without going over it. Controls:A Button: CutTips: Cut it as close to the line as you can for bigger points. If you go over it, you'll lose points.
***Furret's Frolic***
Pokeballs will move into the group of Furrets. Throw it into the grass next to your goal and then into it for points. Get as many points as you can in the time allowed. Controls: Control Stick: MoveA Button: Throw BallTips: Get as many balls as you can into your goal. The bigger the ball, the more points it's worth so go for them the second you see them.
***Barrier Ball***
Play as a Mr. Mime and smash the ball into your opponents court. You'll get points for scoring on them and lose them if they score on you.Controls:Control Stick: Move left or rightA: Smash the ballTips: Watch the balls carefully because sometimes they will curve suddenly and come towards your goal. Don't worry so much about getting goals as protecting yours as you'll lose whatever points you've gotten if one gets in.
***Pichu's Power Plant***
Try and power up your Pichu faster than your opponents. Light bulbs will appear around you and you must absorb their energy.Controls: Control Pad: Move the angle that you absorbA/B Buttons: Press to absorb energy (A Button for a blue bulb, B for a green bulb)Tips: Press your buttons as fast as you can to absorb more energy. Quickly move towards a new bulb as soon as it appears.
***Rampage Rollout***
Control a Donphan and race against others around the track. You'll get dust tornadoes if you're in any place but 1st. Use them to attack your opponents. Controls:Control Pad: MoveA Button: Use a dust tornadoTips: You'll get one tornado for 2nd, two for 3rd, and three for 4th. Lay them on the track if another Donphan is right behind you. Try to stay on the inside of the track to get faster laps. Don't run behind other Donphans if they have tornadoes.
***Streaming Stampede***
You'll either be a Igglybuff or a Cleffa. Before each round, you'll be told the name of the Pokemon you are looking for. Press A Button for each one that goes by. The closer you are to the right answer, the more points you'll get. Controls:A Button: CountTips: Be very careful in later rounds because sometimes you'll have to look for two types of Pokemon or some others will show up and mess you up.
***Tumbling Togepi***
You must help Togepi get to the finish line while avoiding flowers, rocks, and logs on the way down. Step on an arrow to get a burst of speed. Controls: Control Stick: MoveTips: Hit as many arrows as you can to get ahead of your opponents. You can jump over the logs, but you slow down. ***Delibird's Delivery***
Compete against other Delibirds to grab gifts and to take them to the delivery line. Grab two of the same gifts in a row to get more points. Avoid the Swinubs or you'll lose all of your gifts. The points are as follows:Bubble Gum 10Stocking 50Game Boy 100Nintendo 64 250Snorlax Doll 350Piano 600Diamond Ring 1000Controls:Control Stick: MoveTips: Don't grab a lot of gifts or you will move a lot slower. Time yourself moving upscreen to avoid the Swinubs. Get the most points to win.
***Egg Emergency***
Play as a Chansey and collect eggs falling from the sky. Get the most eggs to win while avoiding Voltorbs. There are 100 in all. Controls: L & R Buttons: Move left and rightTips: Avoid the Voltorbs or you'll lose eggs. Move very quickly to get them all.
***Eager Eevee***
Eevees run around a path as an Aipom lifts a cover up and down revealing food. Get the most food to win.Points:Melon 1000Grapes 500Strawberry 100 eachPineco PenaltyControls:A Button: DashB Button: FeintTips: Grab as much fruit as you can, but Feint dash if you see a Pineco or you'll be penalized.
---Mini-Game Champion---
The computer will select who goes first and then that person will get to roll a die. Each side corresponds with a different consequence, either offering coins to the winner, or making the previous winner be forced to win or his opponents get coins. The winner gets to select the mini-game you play. If you become the champion, and have your Gold/Silver pak in the Transfer Pak, you win Coins to use in the Arcade.
---1P Quiz---
Select a difficulty and try to answer as many questions as you can in 100 seconds. If you answer a question wrong, you're penalized 10 seconds. Try to break the records of 25 on Easy, 15 on Normal, and 5 on Hard.
***Easy***
Questions consist of shadows, evolution, name, color questions.
***Normal***
Questions get a little trickier and add more trivia about the Gold/Silver games.
***Hard***
If you want to win this round, you better be up on your Gold/Silver trivia. Questions are about Gym Leaders, Pokedex, moves, and areas of the game. Color questions also try to trick you.
---Quiz For All---
Assortment of all kinds of questions for two through four players. Be the first to answer ten questions to win.
