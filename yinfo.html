Yellow Information

Release Dates

October 1999 (US)

Pokemon Yellow or Pokemon Pikachu is the next series after Pokemon R/B. Pokemon Yellow will be released for Gameboy Color. Unlike Pokemon Red & Blue, Yellow follows the footsteps of the TV Anime series. Which means you start off with Pikachu and your rival Gary Oak starts off with a Eevee. Therefore means you can catch Bulbasuar, Squirtle and Charmander! Also stated before Pikachu will be your starting Pokemon and you can also see him following you. You can look at Pikachu at anythime and see how he feels. Also you can listem to sounds Pikachu makes. In the Yellow Version they have changed Pokemon's evolution to different levels and redesigned certain TMs to different Pokemon. Some images of Pokemon have changed and also now they have added Jessie and James as Team Rocket into the game. Your first encounter with them will be in Mt. Moon. A new function NINTENDO ADDED TO Yellow is the ability to make stickers of your Pokemon using the Gameboy Printer!

New Stuff

  • You don't get to choose a Pokemon in the start. You'll just get Pikachu
  • Gary will get Eevee, just like in the TV Show
  • Team Rocket will be specific members, Jessie and James
  • The new art of the Pokemon will look more like the Pokemon from the TV show
  • Link two Gameboys together with Pokemon Yellow, and you can battle

Review

When Pokemon Yellow came out in October 1999, it was a great excite for all Pokemon fans everywhere. Though most of the graphics were like Red and Blue, the new ones were great.

Rating for graphics: 3.5 out of 5

The game play was kind of like Red and Blue... but in Red and Blue, it was fun and challenging (Until you beat the Elite four)

Rating for the game: 4 out of 5

The game was good for a period, but when Gold and Silver came, this game was nothing.
